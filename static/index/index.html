About the terms

• A long position is used when a profit is expected from the growth of a shares.

• A short position is opened in the hope of profiting from a fall in shares.

• Stop-loss is the lower value of share when the position is closed to cut the loss.

• Take-profit is the upper value at which the position is closed to make an instant profit.
